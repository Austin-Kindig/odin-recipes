Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe is a simple yet tasty staple in the Kindig household!

Ingredients:

  • 2 sticks butter
  • 1 C sugar
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 C flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Steps:

  1. cream in butter and sugar
  2. add dry to a separate bowl (except flour) and mix
  3. combine and mix
  4. add chocolate chips or other add ins
  5. bake at 375 for 10 min on a parchment paper
